Copy and paste best practices

Pro Tip: When writing content, it is a great practice to first write content in a document like microsoft word or google docs. this will help with spellchecking and being able to look at content crirically before pasting and thinking about formatting.

When pasting text from a Word document or from NYU Classes, some of the documentâ€™s text styling will copy over. This will clash with the styles that are in the template, so you should always paste without formatting!

To paste text without formatting, you can use Ctrl+Shift+V (Cmd+Shift+V on Mac) to paste copied text as unformatted text to HTML editor.
